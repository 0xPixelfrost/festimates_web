Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Festimates app (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices, created by Martin Jäger - Festimates (hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider") as a freemium service. This service is provided "AS IS".

1. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users within the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR: Performance of a contract (to provide app features such as Crew-Sync, lineup planning, reward collection, and account management).
  • Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR: Legitimate interests (app diagnostics, analytics, and security).

2. Information Collection and Use

Information Provided by the User: The Application collects information that you provide through Social Login (e.g., via Google, Apple, or other supported providers). Since the core functions of the Application (such as Crew-Sync, lineup planning, and artist rankings) are based on cross-platform synchronization, using the Application without such a login is not possible. Upon registration, basic profile information (such as your email address and name/avatar) is transferred from your chosen provider to the Service Provider to create your account.

Additional User-Generated Content and Activities: The Application also collects and stores information that you actively create within the app, such as names of your crews, festival selections made, and the status of your virtual point system. This data is stored in your user profile to enable synchronization between your devices and within your crew.

The Service Provider may also use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide important information, required notices, or marketing promotions.

Automatically Collected Information: In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, the unique device ID, the IP address (technically required for communication with our cloud services), your mobile operating system, technical connection identifiers, and information about how you use the Application.

3. Location Data and Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Does the Application collect precise real-time location data of the device?
This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Does the Application use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies?
The Application does not use AI technologies to process your data or provide functions.

4. Third-Party Access and Disclosure of Information

Do third parties have access to the information obtained by the Application?
Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to help the Service Provider improve the Application and service. The Service Provider may share your information with third parties in the ways described in this privacy policy.

Please note that the Application uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies. Below are the links to the policies of the service providers used:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config)
  • Supabase (Database, Authentication & Cloud Functions)
  • RevenueCat (In-app Purchases & Subscriptions)

Special Note on In-App Purchases: We use the RevenueCat service to manage purchases. Information regarding your purchase history, such as transaction IDs and unlocked products, is processed and linked to your user account to ensure access to your purchased content.

The Service Provider may disclose information provided by the user and automatically collected information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process;
  • when they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
  • to trusted service providers (see above) who work on their behalf, have no independent use of the information disclosed to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy policy.

5. Opt-Out Rights

You can easily stop all collection of information by the Application by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstallation processes of your mobile device or the app store.

6. Data Retention and Management

The Service Provider stores data provided by the user for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. Automatically collected information is stored for up to 24 months and thereafter kept in aggregated form if necessary.

How can I manage or delete my information?
According to the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data. We offer the following options:

  • Full Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your entire account and all associated data (including your credentials and point balance) directly in the Application settings. This process is irreversible. Please note: Information about in-app purchases made will be decoupled from personal data and stored in anonymized form to comply with legal retention obligations and enable statistical analysis.
  • Profile Reset (Partial Deletion): You can reset your festival history. This irrevocably deletes your lineups, rankings, crew information, and all group memberships. Your login account remains, but existing purchases will be anonymized/decoupled.
  • Manual Requests: If you wish to request the deletion of your data, please contact us at support@festimates.app.

Note on Offline Synchronization: Due to the offline-first nature of the Application, data on the devices of other users (crews) is only removed through synchronization. Once you request deletion, your data will be immediately marked for deletion and will no longer be visible. A technical copy of the marked data will be kept on our servers for up to 30 days to ensure that all offline devices of your crew members receive the deletion command before the data is permanently deleted.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service Provider does not knowingly use the Application to solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. The Application is not addressed to persons under 13 (intended audience is 18+). If the Service Provider discovers that a child under 13 has provided personal data, it will be deleted immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware of this, please contact us at support@festimates.app.

8. Security

The Service Provider is committed to protecting the confidentiality of your information. We employ physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards. For example, all data is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) during transmission between the Application and our cloud providers (Supabase and Firebase). Please note that no security system is absolutely infallible.

9. Changes and Consent

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The Service Provider will inform you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and, if necessary, indicating it within the Application. It is recommended to check this page regularly for changes. Consent: By using the Application and creating an account, you consent to the processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy. "Processing" means using or handling information in any way (including, but not limited to, collecting, storing, deleting, using, combining, and disclosing). We use local storage technologies (such as SQLite/Drift) for offline functionality instead of cookies.

10. Contact

If you have questions about privacy while using the Application or wish to exercise your rights, please contact the Service Provider via email at: support@festimates.app.
