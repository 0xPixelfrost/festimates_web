Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to the Festimates app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Martin Jäger - Festimates (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is provided "AS IS".

1. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users within the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR: Performance of a contract (to provide app features such as Crew-Sync, lineup planning, and account management).
  • Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR: Legitimate interests (app diagnostics, analytics, and security).

2. Information Collection and Use

User Provided Information: The Application collects information that you provide within the scope of Social Login (e.g., via Google, Apple, or other supported providers). Since the core functions of the Application (such as Crew-Sync, lineup planning, and artist rankings) are based on cross-platform synchronization, use of the Application without such a login is not possible. Upon login, basic profile information (such as your email address and your name/avatar) is transferred from your chosen provider to the Service Provider to create your account.

The Service Provider may also use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices, and marketing promotions.

Automatically Collected Information: In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, the unique device ID, the IP address (which is technically required for communication with our cloud services), your mobile operating system, as well as technical connection identifiers and information about the way you use the Application.

3. Location Data and Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?
This Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Does the Application use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies?
The Application does not use AI technologies to process your data or provide features.

4. Third-Party Access and Disclosure of Information

Do third parties have access to information obtained by the Application?
Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to aid the Service Provider in improving the Application and the service. The Service Provider may share your information with third parties in the ways described in this privacy statement.

Please note that the Application utilizes third-party services that have their own privacy policies. Below are the links to the provisions of the utilized service providers:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config)
  • Supabase (Database, Authentication & Cloud Functions)
  • RevenueCat (In-App Purchases & Subscriptions)

The Service Provider may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process;
  • when they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
  • to trusted service providers (see above) who work on their behalf, have no independent use of the information disclosed to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.

5. Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes of your mobile device or the app store for this purpose.

6. Data Retention and Management

The Service Provider will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. Automatically Collected information will be retained for up to 24 months and thereafter may be stored in aggregate form.

How can I manage or delete my information?
According to the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectification, or erasure of your data. We offer the following options:

  • Full Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your entire account and all associated data (including your login credentials) directly within the Application's settings. This process is irreversible. Please note: Information regarding in-app purchases will be decoupled from personal data and retained in anonymized form to comply with statutory retention obligations and to enable statistical evaluations.
  • Profile Reset (Partial Deletion): You can reset your festival history. This irrevocably deletes your lineups, rankings, crew information, as well as all group memberships. Your login account remains, but existing purchases will be anonymized/decoupled.
  • Manual Requests: If you wish to request the deletion of your data, please contact us at support@festimates.app.

Note on Offline Synchronization: Due to the offline-first nature of the Application, data on the devices of other users (crews) is only removed through synchronization. Once you request deletion, your data is immediately marked for deletion and is no longer visible. A technical copy of the marked data is retained on our servers for up to 30 days to ensure that all offline devices of your crew members receive the deletion command before the data is permanently deleted.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service Provider does not knowingly use the Application to solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. The Application is not directed at persons under the age of 13 (the intended target group is 18+). If the Service Provider discovers that a child under 13 has provided personal data, it will be deleted immediately. If you as a parent or guardian become aware of this, please contact us at support@festimates.app.

8. Security

The Service Provider endeavors to protect the confidentiality of your information. We utilize physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards. For example, all data is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) during transmission between the Application and our cloud providers (Supabase and Firebase). Please note that no security system is absolutely infallible.

9. Changes and Consent

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The Service Provider will inform you of any material changes by publishing the new privacy policy on this page and, if applicable, notifying you within the Application. It is recommended to consult this page regularly for changes. Consent: By using the Application and creating an account, you consent to the processing of your information in accordance with this privacy policy. "Processing" means the use of or handling of information in any form (including, but not limited to, collection, storage, deletion, use, combination, and disclosure). We utilize local storage technologies (such as SQLite/Drift) for offline functionality instead of cookies.

10. Contact

If you have questions regarding privacy while using the Application or wish to exercise your rights, please contact the Service Provider via email at: support@festimates.app.
