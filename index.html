You’re standing at the entrance of a massive festival with 150 bands spread across five stages. You’ve spent hours hand-picking your must-see acts, but your friends’ plans remain a total mystery. As soon as you hit the crowd, the network dies. Chat messages hang on "sending," the group scatters, and your coordination vanishes into thin air. Instead of losing yourself in the music, you are left navigating the chaos alone, disconnected from the very people you came to celebrate with.

Festimates was built to end the festival chaos and bring the focus back to the music. Our app is your digital survival guide, designed to work even in the deepest signal black holes with full offline-sync. Discover festivals, mark the artists you live for, and get smart reminders before they hit the stage. But the real magic happens with your Festival Crew: instantly see which bands your friends are watching and where your schedules overlap. No more guessing, no more wandering alone—just perfect "group matches" and shared front-row moments.

Beyond the weekend, Festimates keeps the pulse alive. Stay connected by following your crew's upcoming festival journeys, ensuring you never miss the next legendary road trip. Build your personal musical legacy by rating every performance you witness, creating a permanent archive of your season's highlights. Stop chasing your squad and start living the festival summer you’ve always dreamed of.
